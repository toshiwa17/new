Our History

MCR Sales Company

The company was founded in February 10, 1975 by a group of businessman under the name of MCR Sales Company, by 1991, the company was incorporated under the name of MCR Agriventure Corporation.

MCR Agriventure Corporation

The MCR Agriventure Corporation offers agricultural and veterinary products. It engaged in wholesaling, distribution and retailing of agricultural products and veterinary medicines in all branches.

MCR Branches

Presently, MCR Agriventure Corporation has branches in Bacolod and Cebu with fifteen (18) Retail Trade in: Head Office in Iloilo City, Passi , Dumarao, Roxas City, Sara , Estancia, Balasan, Sibalom & Dalipe Antique , Barotac Nuevo & Viejo , Silay , Cadiz, Sagay Negros Oton Pontevedra Capiz, Escalante Neg Occ. and Victorias . At present, still forecast for expansion places where the need of the company presence is fondled.
